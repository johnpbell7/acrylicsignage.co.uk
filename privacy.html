Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 May 2026

This policy explains what personal information Acrylic Signage (“we”, “us”) collects when you use this website or make an enquiry, how we use it, and the rights you have over it. We are the data controller for the information you provide.

What we collect

When you submit an enquiry or quote through our forms, we collect the details you give us, which may include:

  • Your name;
  • Your email address;
  • Your phone number (if you choose to provide it);
  • The details of your sign or project, including any specifications, dimensions or quotes generated by our calculator.

We do not ask for, or intentionally collect, special category data. Please don’t include sensitive personal information in the free-text fields.

How we use it

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and prepare a quote;
  • Discuss your project, artwork and order with you;
  • Fulfil and deliver any order you go on to place;
  • Keep records of our correspondence and transactions.

Our lawful basis for processing is our legitimate interest in responding to enquiries and running our business, and, where you place an order, the performance of a contract with you.

Who we share it with

We only share your information with service providers that help us operate, such as our enquiry/CRM platform and email provider, and only so they can perform services on our behalf. We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information if required to do so by law.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry information for as long as needed to deal with your request and for a reasonable period afterwards in case you get back in touch. Where you place an order, we keep records as long as necessary to meet our legal and accounting obligations.

Cookies & analytics

This website does not set advertising cookies. If we introduce analytics in future, we will update this policy and, where required, ask for your consent.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information, to object to or restrict its processing, and to data portability. To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@acrylicsignage.co.uk. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Contact

For any questions about this policy or your information, contact us at hello@acrylicsignage.co.uk.

This policy is provided as general information and may need tailoring to your business’s specific circumstances. Consider having it reviewed by a qualified professional before relying on it.
